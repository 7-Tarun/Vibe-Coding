## 🩺 HealthCalc
**Know your body without opening 5 different apps.**

A free, no-signup health & fitness calculator hub — all your everyday health tools in one place.

🔗 [Live Demo](https://7-tarun.github.io/Vibe-Coding/Health-Calculators/)

**Tools included:**
- BMI Calculator
- TDEE Calculator
- BMR Calculator
- Calorie Calculator
- Macro Calculator
- Water Intake Calculator
- Protein Intake, Ideal Weight, Body Fat % *(more tools planned)*

More calculators are being added over time, and an installable (PWA) version is planned so you can use it like a native app.

## 👁️ Eye Rest
**Staring at your screen for hours? Your eyes are begging for a break.**

An Android app built around the scientifically-backed **20-20-20 rule** — every 20 minutes, look 20 feet away for 20 seconds. Simple, but it actually protects your eyes from digital strain.

🔗 [Download & Info](https://7-tarun.github.io/Vibe-Coding/Eye-Break/)

**Features:**
- ⏱️ Adjustable timer intervals — not locked to just 20 minutes
- ✍️ Custom notification text — set your own reminder message
- 🔕 Sound toggle — silent reminders for quiet spaces
- 🔄 Runs in the background as a foreground service, even if you swipe the app away

Requirements: Android 8.0+ · Size: ~1.33 MB
