About HealthCalc

HealthCalc is a free online health calculator website built to help you understand your body better. Our tools provide quick estimates for BMI, TDEE, calorie needs, macros, and body fat so you can make smarter wellness decisions.

Our Purpose

We want health metrics to feel simple and accessible for everyone. HealthCalc was created to give you quick access to BMI, TDEE, calorie, macro, and other wellness calculations without any login, ads, or confusing charts.

Whether you are tracking weight goals, starting a fitness plan, or planning your daily calorie intake, our tools provide transparent results you can trust.

Why Choose Our Calculators?

  • Instant results with clean, mobile-friendly design.
  • Built using trusted equations like Mifflin-St Jeor and BMI formulas.
  • Private experience: no account required and no personal data collection.
  • Helpful guidance so you can interpret your results safely.

Our Promise

We believe wellness tools should be simple, reliable, and respectful of your privacy. HealthCalc is committed to delivering useful health estimates while keeping your experience safe, fast, and distraction-free.
