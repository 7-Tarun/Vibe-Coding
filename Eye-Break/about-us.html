About Us

Welcome to Eye Rest.

Built by Tarun, a developer who understands the physical toll of long programming and study sessions, this project was created to solve a real-world problem: Digital Eye Strain.

We noticed that most screen break timers are highly intrusive, locking your device or requiring complex setups. Our mission is to provide a minimalist, highly efficient, and privacy-respecting tool that seamlessly integrates the scientifically-backed 20-20-20 rule into your daily workflow.

Our focus is strictly on functionality, user control, and ocular health.
